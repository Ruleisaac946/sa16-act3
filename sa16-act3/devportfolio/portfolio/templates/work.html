My Work

Over the years, I've been fortunate to contribute to a wide range of projects, each unique in its challenges and successes. Below are highlights of some of the key projects I've developed or contributed to, showcasing my technical skills and project management capabilities.

E-commerce Platform

This project involved developing a robust e-commerce platform for a retail client looking to expand their online presence. Utilizing Ruby on Rails and React.js, the platform supports thousands of transactions daily, features a dynamic recommendation engine, and integrates seamless payment processing solutions.

Technologies used: Ruby on Rails, React.js, PostgreSQL, Stripe API

Achievement: Boosted client's sales by 120% within the first year of launch.

Content Management System for News Network

Designed and implemented a custom content management system for a major news network, enhancing the efficiency of content delivery and enabling real-time updates. The system features a user-friendly interface and robust backend capable of handling large volumes of data.

Technologies used: Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, Redis

Achievement: Improved editorial efficiency by 40% and reduced content update downtimes by 75%.

Mobile Fitness Application

Developed a mobile fitness application that personalizes workout and nutrition plans based on user inputs and goals. The app includes social sharing features, progress tracking, and integrates with wearable devices for real-time health monitoring.

Technologies used: Node.js, React Native, MongoDB, Firebase

Achievement: Reached 100,000 active users within six months of launch.
